Diogo F. Soares is an Invited Assistant Professor at the Department of Informatics, Faculty of Sciences, University of Lisbon, and a researcher at LASIGE, contributing to the Health and Biomedical Informatics and Data and Systems Intelligence research lines. He teaches courses in Programming and Data Science and holds a PhD in Informatics and an MSc in Data Science from the University of Lisbon. His research focuses on patient-centered machine learning, with involvement in European and National funded projects. His primary research interests include Data Mining, Machine Learning, Unsupervised Learning Algorithms, and Biomedical Informatics.
